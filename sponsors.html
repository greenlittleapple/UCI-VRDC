The HTC Vive is the premier VR headset from Valve using SteamVR software. In addition to tracked haptic feedback controllers and a 2160 x 1200 resolution, the Vive possesses full 360 degree room-scale tracking with 6 degrees of freedom for limitless movement up to 15 feet.

UCI Esports is the first of its kind collegiate esports program, officially backed by the University of Irvine. Founded in 2016, the program includes scholarships for its elite League of Legends and Counter-Strike: Global Offensive competitors, a 3,500 square feet Esports Arena with 72 iBUYPOWER computers, and countless research into the field of computer game science.

Learn more on the official UCI Esports website!

The Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences is UC Irvine's designated school of computer science. Founded in 2002, the School of ICS was established as the first UC computer science school not under Engineering. This attribute has allowed UC Irvine to thrive in computer science; in addition to offering informative classes and unique specializations, the school performs daily research in fields like artificial intelligence, human-computer interaction, and virtual reality.

Mecha Mayhem is an open world action adventure VR game that can be played on the Oculus Rift and HTC Vive. The game is set in a post apocalyptic future where resources have been exhausted and Mechs reign supreme. There exists different factions from all over the world pitted against each other in an endless battle for world domination.
