About VR@UCI

  We are a group of Virtual Reality enthusiasts dedicated to spreading VR awareness and developing VR projects! In Winter 2018, we formed Virtual Reality at UCI as a first of its kind student VR organization at UC Irvine to bring our enthusiasm to our fellow students. We have since established ourselves as a core part of the gaming community, created our first VR game project, hosted several VR awareness events, and cemented ourselves as the biggest VR group on campus, even being sponsored by multiple organizations!



As a multipurpose organization, we have several types of meetings and events:

General Meetings - (Tentative) Fridays at 7PM in ICS 195
We discuss club events and projects, and talk about VR in general.

We work on the club project.
