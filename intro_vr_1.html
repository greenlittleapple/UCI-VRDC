What is Virtual Reality?


Virtual Reality is a rapidly emerging medium of immersive technology that allows users to engage in
virtual worlds using Head Mounted Displays (HMD) and three dimensional space.

VR technology is being used to revolutionize many industries such as the Medical, Entertainment, Gaming, Engineering and many more.

To create immersive, compelling content for VR we must utilize tried and true game design principles and precise engineering of devices.
One of our goals at VRDC is to experiment with VR development to learn the workflow of this new medium.
