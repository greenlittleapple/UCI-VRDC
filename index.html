About VRUCI

Virtual Reality UCI is a first of its kind, student-oriented, campus organization at UC Irvine focused on the development and exploration of virtual reality, augmented reality, and more. Our club welcomes students from all backgrounds to discover new facets (? could be worded better)of VR technology. We provide a strong platform of both knowledge and hardware for students to develop virtual reality applications. Whether you are brand new to VR or are a VR expert, you will be able to find students to work alongside.
