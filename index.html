About VRUCI

        Virtual Reality at UCI (VRUCI) is a student-oriented campus organization at UC Irvine focused on the exploration and development of virtual and augmented reality. We welcome students of all interests and skills to discuss, learn about, and create ideas for VR at our weekly meetings. Through our workshops and resources, we strive to provide a strong platform of both knowledge and hardware for students to develop for the future.

        In addition to developing projects, VRUCI also collaborates with research labs, UCI Esports, and other clubs on and off campus in order to deliver meaningful virtual reality experiences and events. Every quarter, we host several VR play and social events called VRcades and collaborate with school events such as ICS Day and ZotCon. Our most recent collaboration was at HackUCI 2019, where we fostered VR community growth by giving a VR workshop and mentoring VR hacks.
