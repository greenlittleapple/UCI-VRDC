Join VRDC!

By joining VRDC, you gain access to many benefits including:
  ☆ The ability to greatly change the face of gaming at UCI!
  ☆ A community of friendly, motivated VR enthusiasts
  ☆ Access to project meetings, special events, and more~

We want you to be able to participate in VRDC without having to use anything besides your free time. As such, we've ensured that there are no barriers for your membership:
  - There are no club fees or dues.
  - There is no knowledge or skill requirement (although we highly recommend you come in with an open mind).

All you need to do is fill out the form and attend meetings!
